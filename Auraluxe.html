About Us

We are based in Newcastle, KZN (for now).

We’re tired of products that look great but don’t last — or ones that work well but look boring. So we created the perfect balance: clean design + reliable performance.

Our products are made for real people: students, young professionals, creators, and everyone who wants tech that’s both practical and aesthetic.

Auraluxe isn’t just a brand — it’s a lifestyle brand built around clarity, comfort, and confidence.

Our Mission

To create stylish, dependable gadgets that deliver a clear, premium experience without a premium price tag.

Our Vision

To become the go-to brand for people who want technology that blends high performance with everyday style.

Our Values

  • Honesty: If we don’t love it, we don’t sell it.
  • Quality: Built to last, not just to look good.
  • Clarity: Straightforward products, straightforward prices.
  • Style: Minimal, modern, and easy to pair with anything.
  • Care: We listen, improve, and put customers first.

Founder’s Message — Alwande Radebe
